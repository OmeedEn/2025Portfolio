# Resume Setup

To enable resume downloads on your portfolio, place your resume PDF file in this directory.

## Required File:

- `resume.pdf` - Your resume in PDF format

## Instructions:

1. **Export your resume as PDF**
   - Make sure it's named exactly `resume.pdf`
   - Keep the file size reasonable (ideally under 2MB)

2. **Place the file in the public folder**
   - Copy your `resume.pdf` to: `/public/resume.pdf`

3. **The download will work automatically**
   - When users click "Download Resume", it will download as `Omeed_Enshaie_Resume.pdf`

## Where the Download Button Appears:

1. **Home Page** - Below the navigation buttons (Experience, Projects, Education, Contact)
2. **Contact Page** - At the bottom of the contact section

## Updating Your Resume:

Simply replace the `resume.pdf` file with your updated version. The changes will be reflected immediately on your portfolio.

## Tips:

- Keep your resume updated with your latest experience
- Ensure the PDF is properly formatted and readable
- Test the download on different browsers to ensure compatibility
- Consider using a professional resume template for best results
